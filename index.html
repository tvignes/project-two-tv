About Us

Baby's Best King Cakes is a locally owned bakery in Baton Rouge Louisiana. We started off as a Mardi Gras-loving family making king cakes in the kitchen, and in 2024 we were able to open our storefront. Our number one priority is making sure you have access to Louisiana's favorite cake all year round.
